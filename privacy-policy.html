Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Ghollak ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal finance management app developed by Elyas Naranjee Sani. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Ghollak app.

Information We Collect

Ghollak is designed to work entirely on your device. The app does not require an account or sign-in, and we do not collect or transmit your personal financial data to any external server.

The following data may be collected anonymously and is not linked to your identity:

  • Crash data and diagnostics — to help us identify and fix bugs and improve stability.
  • App usage data — anonymous analytics about how features are used, to help us improve the experience.

Financial Data

All financial information you enter into Ghollak — including accounts, transactions, and balances — is stored locally on your device and is never sent to our servers. We have no access to your financial data.

Exported Files

Ghollak allows you to export your data as PDF and Excel files. These files are created on your device and shared at your discretion. We do not receive or store any exported files.

Face ID / Biometric Authentication

If you choose to enable Face ID protection, this is handled entirely by your device's operating system. We do not have access to any biometric data.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. The app may use Apple's standard frameworks for analytics and crash reporting, subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Ghollak is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at: ghollak.app/#contact
