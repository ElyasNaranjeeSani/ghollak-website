Privacy Policy

یادآور تولد شمسی — Persian Solar Birthday Reminder

Last updated: May 2025

Tavallod ("we", "our", or "us") is a Persian solar calendar birthday reminder app developed by Elyas Naranjee Sani. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the Tavallod app.

Information We Collect

Tavallod is a local app — all data you enter stays on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal information. The following data may be collected anonymously:

  • Crash data and diagnostics — to help us identify bugs and improve stability.
  • App usage data — anonymous analytics to understand feature usage and improve the experience.

This data is not linked to your identity in any way.

Contacts and Birthday Data

Tavallod may request access to your device's Contacts in order to help you add birthdays more quickly. This access is optional — you can use the app without granting it. Contact data is only read on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Birthday entries you create manually are stored locally on your device and are not synced to or accessible by us.

Notifications

Tavallod uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming birthdays. These notifications are scheduled on your device and do not involve any external server communication.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes. The app may use Apple's standard frameworks for crash reporting and analytics, subject to Apple's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Tavallod is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at: ghollak.app/#contact
